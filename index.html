About Us

PT. Lomboo Handicraft Industry or commonly known as Lomboo Handicraft is a company engaged in the handicraft industry as well as an exporter of Indonesian crafts. Our products are made of natural materials such as rattan, wicker, ketak/ate, bamboo, wood, cukli, and pottery/terra-cotta. The products we produce are perfect for accessories, home furnishings, art decorations, and also interior design.
